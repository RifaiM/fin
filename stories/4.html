Chapter 1

Dear Cita,

how are you? ..hope you always get healthy ..hope this email finds you well too

you know? i'm getting to lost the reason, for every decision that i've made through till' this time. my head full of stuff, about everything ..but the time sure does not wait, to let me think or at least to let me enjoy sleeping

but there's one time ..when i finally got a well sleep, even it's just a minutes or two (can't remember) ..i'm dreaming, you're out there ..with your calm and warmth smile, sure ..i can feel it. am i ..am i really meet you back then? or is that just a glimpse of vision, of the future? then i suddenly wake up

tears ..really, stream down my face (like a coldplay song) ..i don't know why, sorry .. it's weird, cause i don't even know ..how's your feel out there, to me.. don't want to say, but i already enjoying the time ..the silence ..every time i'm writing you a letter, so ..i won't ..give up

that time ..i still remember it well, do you?

last, i know for sure, you've done well of everything ..don't give up.. rest enough at least for this day then give your best everytime you've made out to wake up ya..

see you.. in the million soon cita,
